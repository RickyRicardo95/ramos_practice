Lil Ram Ram

Lil Ram Ram was a raptor on Ark, was a great raptor... A loyal raptor. One day, on a daily stroll through the wilderness, there was a griefing tribe that ran up on us. I remember the screams of when we first spotted their warship just off the coast of the People Eating People base. It was a simultaneous screech in the air that we looked up and to our dismay we saw the horible sight of two Argentavis'. The two Argentavis' picked up both the Lil Ram Ramo and Ramo, they ascended to an insane altitude and did the unthinkable. I think you can guess what happened next, but needless to say poor Lil Ram Ram did not survive the fall of this horrid terrorist attack. And to this day, we will never forget you Lil Ram Ram.. R.I.P. Little buddy.
